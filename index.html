Hi, I’m Florent, welcome to my personal website! I recently finished my PhD in Computer Science at LIPN, the CS lab of Université Sorbonne Paris Nord (Paris 13), France. My broad areas of interest are unsupervised machine learning and large-scale data-driven applications. I like to develop and apply advanced algorithms on large, complex industrial data sets. The PhD project was an industry collaboration with Safran Aircraft Engines, a leading aircraft engine manufacturer. Before, I graduated from ISAE-Supaero engineering school in Toulouse, France. You can find recent news and events on this page!
